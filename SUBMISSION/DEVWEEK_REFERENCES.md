## Submission Facts To Keep Current

- **Deadline:** July 21, 2026 at 5:00 PM PDT.
- **Track:** PITT belongs in **Work and Productivity**.
- **Working project:** required; it must run consistently on its stated platform and match the video/text description.
- **Video:** required; public YouTube, under three minutes, with a clear working demo and audio covering what was built plus how Codex and GPT-5.6 were used.
- **Repository:** required; public with appropriate licensing, or private and shared with `testing@devpost.com` and `build-week-event@openai.com`.
- **README:** must explain how Codex accelerated the work, key product/engineering/design decisions, and how GPT-5.6 and Codex contributed.
- **Testing access:** provide a running website, functioning demo, or test build; make it free and available to judges through the judging period.
- **Codex evidence:** include the `/feedback` Codex session ID where most core functionality was built.

## PITT Submission Positioning

PITT is a runnable, local web demo for the **Work and Productivity** track. It demonstrates a seeded delivery-planning and trip-exception workflow; it does not claim live fleet integrations. The narrative should use the product direction to explain why the workflow could matter commercially, while the video and README stay anchored to the actual simulated POC.
