## Final audio contract

- Every cue has 348–363 ms of measured silence before its first sound.
- All eleven cues were transcribed individually before assembly and again scene by scene from the final AAC track.
- `Pitt` is recognized consistently; the abbreviation is never spelled aloud.
- Every final cue measures between -17.4 and -18.8 LUFS integrated; the opening is exactly -18.0 LUFS.
- True peaks are at or below -1.5 dB.
- A 1.04x final tempo adjustment keeps the complete cut under three minutes without changing pitch.
- Captions use the branded spelling `PITT` and are embedded as English `mov_text`.
