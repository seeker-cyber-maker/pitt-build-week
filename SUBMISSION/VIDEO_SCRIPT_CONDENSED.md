- Use hard cuts between the four outcomes. Put a small `1 / 4` through `4 / 4` label in the same corner.

Reuse the existing recording of Codex recording the video even if the webpage shown inside it predates later product changes—the collaboration process, not that nested webpage state, is the subject of the shot.
